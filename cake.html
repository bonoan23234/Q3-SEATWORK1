Fiona and Cake

Adventure Time is an animated series that follows the adventures of Finn (human) and his Jake(magical dog) In the alternate universe, Fiona and Cake take center stage.

Info about the two!

Fiona

Fiona is the gender swapped version of Finn. She is a brave and adventurous character with her trusty sword.

Cake

Cake is the gender swapped version of Jake the magical dog but instead of a dog it's a cat now. She is a stretchy cat with the ability to shape shift into various forms.
